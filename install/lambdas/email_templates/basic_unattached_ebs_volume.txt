
Unattached EBS Volumes
The account has EBS volumes which are not attached to an instance. 
It's recommended to check if the unattached EBS volumes are needed.  If they are not, consider deleting them.
https://docs.aws.amazon.com/prescriptive-guidance/latest/optimize-costs-microsoft-workloads/ebs-delete-ebs-volumes.html
